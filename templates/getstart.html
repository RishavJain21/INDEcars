Terminology

Let's get you acquainted with some of the terms about cars

  • Make: The "Make" of a car is its brand
  • Model: The model is the name of a specific vehicle of a brand
  • Mileage: The number of miles or the average distance that a vehicle can travel on a specified quantity of fuel.
  • Ground clearance: The amount of clear space between the lowest part of a vehicles frame and the ground.
  • Wheelbase: The distance between the front and rear axles of a vehicle.
  • Auto Dimming Rear-View_Mirror: It consists of a mirror element and an electronic system that detects light from in front and behind by means of photo sensors
  • Drivetrain: The system in a motor vehicle which connects the transmission to the drive axles
  • Emission Norm: Emission norms that set the maximum permissible levels for pollutants that an automotive or a two-wheeler exhaust can emit.
